Professional Florida Process Service

Process servers play a crucial role in the justice system as they notify an individual of their involvement in a court proceeding. This is one of the first steps in initiating a legal case. Service of process should be carried out by a professional server.

Our Florida process servers adhere to all local and state laws to ensure acceptable service that holds up in court. We deliver all types of legal documents including subpoenas, summons, complaints, writs, and more. We offer routine and rush services across the state of Florida. We also offer our services Nationwide, we can get your legal documents served whenever and wherever you need.

Whether you need a Florida process server or a document served across the country, Florida Serves, Inc has you covered! Upload your documents using our online order form and from there, you will always be informed of the status of your case.

Other Services

Florida Serves tackles all your legal needs. From notarizing documents and serving process to finding missing parties and filing court docs, we handle it all efficiently and reliably.

Mobile Notary

Our trained and professional notaries will certify the signatures on your important legal documents. This includes property deeds, wills, loan documents, and more. We serve as an impartial witness to ensure that all signatures belong to the proper party and are completed with intimidation. Our Florida notaries are mobile meaning we will travel to you!

Courier Service​

Our process servers are accustomed to handling sensitive legal documents. You can trust us to pick up and deliver your other important parcels as well including mail, packages, and messages. We are familiar with our local area which ensures a quick and efficient delivery process. You can trust Florida Serves, Inc with your legal courier and messenger needs.

Skip Tracing

When a defendant cannot be found or an address turns out incorrect, we perform a skip trace to locate the individual to properly serve them legal documents. We use modern databases and in-depth research to complete this service. If you have someone who is evading service, we have the tools to find them quickly so your case can in a timely fashion.

Court Filing​

Our team is familiar with our local Florida courts and the different requirements of various case types. We will submit your legal documents whether it be by eFiling or in person in order to supplement your legal case. We provide these services accurately and efficiently in order to meet your specified deadlines. We can both file and retrieve documents within Florida's court systems.
